Choctaw Indian Princess
Shemah Ladania Crosby

Shemah Ladania Crosby is from the Standing Pine Community and is the 19-year- old daughter of Oga Crosby and Lalania Denson. Her grandparents are Beasley and the late Lena Denson and Phillip and the late Robbye Crosby. She has two siblings, Laliana and Yuriana. Shemah also has two pets, a cat named Brees (after Drew Brees) and a white German Shepherd named Daya.

A 2020 graduate of Choctaw Central High School, Shemah is currently a sophomore studying anthropology at Mississippi State University. Her hobbies include beadwork making, modeling, getting nails done, golfing and gardening. Shemah’s favorite Choctaw cultural tradition is beadwork making, especially making medallion earrings.
